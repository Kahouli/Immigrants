My Experience

Reasons

What made you quit the place you were living in ? Were you just looking for a new and better life, maybe you were forced to leave because of a conflict, or maybe it was love ?

Process

Talking about the moment when you realize that you re leaving your home. Describing the journey to get to your destination. And getting ready physically and mentally to live a new life.

Result

Was emigratting the right decision ? Where you welcomed in your new society ? Do you consider settling there ? And Do you have any advice for the future immigrants ?

Stories

Why should I write my story ?

Let your experience be a lessons for others.

By sharing your story, you will be helping some people to have a much clear vision about what will happen to them. And guide them to make sure they don't do the same mistakes you did.
